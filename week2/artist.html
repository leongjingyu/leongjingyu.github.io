IU(Lee Ji-eun)

IU (Lee Ji-eun), born on May 16, 1993, is a South Korean singer-songwriter and actress. She debuted in 2008 under EDAM Entertainment and gained popularity through her powerful vocals, emotional songwriting, and unique musical style. IU is widely recognized as one of South Korea’s most successful solo artists, with hit songs such as “Good Day,” “Palette,” and “Through the Night.” Besides her music career, she has also built a strong reputation as an actress through dramas including Hotel Del Luna and My Mister. Known for her warm personality, creativity, and dedication to her fans, IU has earned numerous awards and has become an influential figure in the Korean entertainment industry.
